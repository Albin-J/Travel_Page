KERALA

For many travelers, Kerala is South India's most serenely beautiful state.

This slender coastal strip is defined by its layered landscape: almost

373 miles (600km) of glorious Arabian Sea coast and beaches;a languid network of glistening backwaters;

wildlife and the spice- & tea-covered hills of the Western Ghats,dotted with fiercely protected reserves

reserves and cool hill stations such as Munnar. Just setting foot on this swathe of soul-soothing, palm-

shaded green will slow your subcontinental stride to a blissed-out amble.Kerala is a world away from the

hectic action of the rest of India,its long, fascinating backstory illuminated by historically evocative

cities like Kochi (Cochin) and Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum).
